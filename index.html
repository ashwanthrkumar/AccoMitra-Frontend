ABOUT ACCOMITRA

Connecting Businesses with Trusted Accountants Across India

AccoMitra is India’s first online marketplace built exclusively for accountants and businesses. We help companies, startups, and individuals connect with verified accounting professionals for services like GST filing, bookkeeping, tax consultation, audits, and more.

Whether you’re a small business looking for reliable accounting help or an accountant seeking new clients — AccoMitra is your trusted digital partner.

Our vision is to create a transparent, efficient, and tech-driven ecosystem where businesses can easily access accounting expertise on demand.

We aim to empower accountants across India with digital visibility, growth opportunities, and tools that make collaboration seamless and impactful.

AccoMitra started with a simple realization — finding a reliable accountant or managing clients as one shouldn’t be hard in the digital age.

From that idea grew a platform where technology bridges the gap between accountants and businesses. Today, AccoMitra continues to grow with one goal: to make accounting accessible, transparent, and stress-free for everyone.
